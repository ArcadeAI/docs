The Gmail toolkit provides a comprehensive set of tools for interacting with Gmail through its API. Users can easily manage their email communications and drafts, allowing them to:

- Send, reply to, and manage emails and drafts.
- Search for and list emails and threads based on various criteria.
- Organize emails with labels and manage draft emails.
- Retrieve user profile information and account statistics.

This toolkit simplifies email management and enhances productivity for users and applications.
