# X Tweets Tools

**Description:** Tools for tweets in the X toolkit

## PostTweet

Post a tweet to X (Twitter).

### **Parameters:**

**tweet_text**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">string</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-required">Required</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  
The text content of the tweet you want to post

---

## DeleteTweetById

### **Parameters:**

**tweet_id**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">string</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-required">Required</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

---

## SearchRecentTweetsByUsername

Search for recent tweets (last 7 days) on X (Twitter) by username. Includes replies and reposts.

### **Parameters:**

**username**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">string</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-required">Required</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  
The username of the X (Twitter) user to look up
<hr style={{borderTop: '0.5px solid #888', margin: '10px 0'}} />
**max_results**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">integer</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-optional">Optional</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-default">Defaults to 10</span>  
The maximum number of results to return. Cannot be less than 10

---

## SearchRecentTweetsByKeywords

Search for recent tweets (last 7 days) on X (Twitter) by required keywords and phrases. Includes replies and reposts
One of the following input parametersMUST be provided: keywords, phrases

### **Parameters:**

**keywords**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">array</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-required">Required</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-default">Defaults to None</span>  
List of keywords that must be present in the tweet
<hr style={{borderTop: '0.5px solid #888', margin: '10px 0'}} />
**phrases**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">array</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-required">Required</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-default">Defaults to None</span>  
List of phrases that must be present in the tweet
<hr style={{borderTop: '0.5px solid #888', margin: '10px 0'}} />
**max_results**&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-type">integer</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-optional">Optional</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span className="tool-param-default">Defaults to 10</span>  
The maximum number of results to return. Cannot be less than 10
