## ListDocuments

List documents in the user's Google Drive. Excludes documents that are in the trash.

**Auth:**

- **Scopes Required:**
  - `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly`

**Environment Variables:**

- None

**Parameters**

- **`corpora`** *(enum ([Corpora](/integrations/toolkits/Google/shared/enums-reference#corpora)), Optional)*:
  - The source of files to list. Defaults to `Corpora.USER`.

- **`order_by`** *(enum ([OrderBy](/integrations/toolkits/Google/shared/enums-reference#orderby)), Optional)*:
  - Sort order. Defaults to `OrderBy.MODIFIED_TIME_DESC`.

- **`supports_all_drives`** *(bool, Optional)*:
  - Whether the application supports both My Drives and shared drives. Defaults to `False`.

- **`limit`** *(int, Optional)*:
  - The number of documents to list. Defaults to `25`.

**Returns**

- **`dict`**: Contains `documents_count` (number of documents returned) and `documents` (a list of document details including `kind`, `mimeType`, `id`, and `name` for each document).
