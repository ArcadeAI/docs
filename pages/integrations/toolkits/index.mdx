# Prebuilt Toolkits

Arcade AI provides ready-to-use toolkits that help you integrate popular services and APIs into your projects. These toolkits are built and maintained by the Arcade AI team to ensure reliability and seamless integration.

You can choose to use Arcade AI's hosted version of these toolkits or self-host them in your own environment.
