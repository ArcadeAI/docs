## Welcome to Arcade AI!

Arcade AI is a developer platform that enables AI applications to interact securely with real-world data and services through authenticated tool-calling. It addresses the challenge of integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) with external systems that require user-specific authentication and action capabilities.
