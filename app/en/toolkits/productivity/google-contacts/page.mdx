The Arcade Google Contacts toolkit provides a pre-built set of tools for interacting with Google Contacts. These tools make it easy to build agents and AI apps that can:

- Create new contacts
- Search for contacts by name or email

## Auth

The Arcade Google Contacts toolkit uses the [Google auth provider](/home/auth-providers/google) to connect to users' Google accounts.

With the Arcade Cloud Platform, there's nothing to configure. Your users will see `Arcade` as the name of the application that's requesting permission.

With a self-hosted installation of Arcade, you need to [configure the Google auth provider](/home/auth-providers/google#configuring-google-auth) with your own Google app credentials.
